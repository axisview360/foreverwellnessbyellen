Ellen accepts the following payment methods: Cash on Delivery (COD) for Davao City meetups and local deliveries, GCash (scan QR code or send to Ellen's registered number), Maya (formerly PayMaya), and Bank Transfer via BDO or BPI. Delivery

Ellen primarily serves Davao City and nearby areas including Davao del Norte (Tagum, Carmen, Sto. Tomas), Digos City, and General Santos. For customers outside these areas, orders can be shipped via LBC or J&T Express with shipping costs shouldered by the buyer. Ellen will give you an accurate shipping estimate before confirming your order.

For Davao City orders, same-day meetup or delivery is usually possible if the order is placed before 3PM. Home delivery within Davao City typically takes 1–2 business days. Nearby provincial areas (Tagum, Digos) take 1–3 days. Shipping via LBC or J&T to other provinces typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Ellen will always give you an estimated timeframe when she confirms your order.
